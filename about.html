About

Designer. Sometimes engineer.

Ninad Kulkarni is a designer and software engineer based in Phoenix, Arizona. His foundation in graphic design began in high school and deepened through two years as a student designer at ASU's Enterprise Marketing Hub, working across print and digital for Athletics and Alumni.

He studied Computer Science at Arizona State University and is currently a full-stack software engineer at Blue Voice. He previously founded Drive Insight, a data visualization platform for vehicle telemetry, where he owned the complete design and brand identity.
