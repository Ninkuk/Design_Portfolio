ASU Alumni + Athletics

From 2019 to 2020, Ninad worked as a graphic designer at Arizona State University's Enterprise Marketing Hub, creating visual assets for the Athletics and Alumni divisions. The work spanned both print and digital — from social media posts and YouTube thumbnails to posters, flyers, and promotional materials. This gallery presents a curated selection of pieces from that period, showcasing breadth across mediums and disciplines. The collection demonstrates the range of design thinking required to support institutional marketing at scale, from quick-turnaround social content to considered print collateral.
