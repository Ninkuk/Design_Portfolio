ASU Alumni + Athletics

From 2019 to 2021 I worked as a student designer at Arizona State University's Enterprise Marketing Hub, making assets for the Athletics and Alumni divisions. The work ranged across print and digital: social posts, YouTube thumbnails, posters, flyers, and promotional collateral. This gallery is a curated slice, weighted toward the pieces I'm still proud of.
