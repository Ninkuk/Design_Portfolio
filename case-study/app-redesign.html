Blue Voice app redesign

Blue Voice is a CJIS-compliant AI platform that provides law enforcement officers with immediate access to legal, policy, and community resources through spoken or typed queries. When I joined in August 2025, I led a comprehensive design modernization: standardizing components, refining the color system, and adding purposeful micro-interactions to elevate the overall experience while preserving the fast, intuitive UX officers rely on.

Problem

The original app interface, while functional, reflected earlier design trends and lacked the visual refinement expected of a modern platform. As Blue Voice scaled and positioned itself competitively, the UX needed a contemporary refresh — standardized components, updated color language, and purposeful micro-interactions that reflected 2025 design standards. The goal was to elevate the user experience without altering the core UX logic that made the app fast and intuitive for officers in the field.

Outcome

The redesigned interface received positive feedback from officers in the field — the modernized visual language and polished interactions made the platform feel more refined and trustworthy. The redesign strengthened Blue Voice's competitive positioning and contributed to sales conversations by presenting a contemporary, professional product. The core efficiency of the original UX remained intact, so officers could still move quickly through queries without friction.
